Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Hubbard Housing Realty Team (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, submit information through our forms, or otherwise interact with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Contact Details: Your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Property Information: Property address, condition, timeline for sale, and any other information you provide about your property.
  • Communications: Content of your messages to us, responses to our messages, and any other communications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide services: We use your information to evaluate properties, make cash offers, schedule walkthroughs, and facilitate the sale of your property.
  • To communicate: We may send you messages by phone, text (SMS), or email with appointment reminders, status updates, and information related to our services.
  • To improve our services: We analyze information to understand and improve our business practices and services.

SMS and Phone Communications

By submitting your phone number through our website or by calling us, you consent to receive SMS or voice messages from Hubbard Housing Realty Team related to our real estate services. Message frequency may vary, and message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, reply HELP or contact us using the methods listed below.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services (e.g., title companies, contractors) or as required by law. These service providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. You can also contact us to update or delete your information. Please note that deleting certain information may limit our ability to provide services.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no security measure is absolute; please use caution when transmitting personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new “Last updated” date.

Seller Situations We Help

Every homeowner's situation is unique. Here are some common scenarios where our cash buying service can provide the perfect solution.

Inherited Property

Dealing with an inherited home? We understand the emotional and logistical challenges. We can help you navigate the process.

Foreclosure

Behind on payments? We may be able to help you avoid foreclosure and protect your credit. Time is critical—contact us immediately.

Relocating

Need to move quickly for work or family? We can close on your timeline, whether that's 7 days or 30+ days.

Major Repairs

Don't want to deal with expensive repairs? We buy homes that need foundation work, new roofs, electrical updates, and more.

Problem Tenants

Tired of dealing with difficult tenants or want to exit the landlord business? We can purchase occupied rental properties.

Code Violations

Facing city code violations or liens? We have experience with these situations and can often help resolve them at closing.

Behind on Payments

Struggling with mortgage payments? We may be able to help you avoid damage to your credit while getting you out from under the debt.

Divorce

Going through a divorce and need to sell the house quickly? We can provide a fast, hassle-free solution during a difficult time.

Don't See Your Situation?

Every situation is different, and we've helped homeowners with all kinds of unique circumstances. If you need to sell your Minnesota home quickly or can't go through the traditional listing process, we'd love to hear from you.

About Hubbard Housing Realty

Local Minnesota Home Buyers You Can Trust

Founded in 2018, Hubbard Housing Realty was created to help Minnesota homeowners who need to sell quickly or can't go through the traditional listing process.

As a family-owned business, we understand that selling a home can be stressful. Whether you're facing foreclosure, dealing with an inherited property, relocating for work, or simply want to avoid the hassle of repairs and showings, we're here to help.

Our team has purchased over 150 homes across Minnesota, and we pride ourselves on honest communication, fair offers, and flexible closing timelines that work for you.
